Project Name

Agu Bot: A Career Guidance Chatbot Aligned with MBTI and Skill Sets

Project Details

This project was conducted as part of an artificial intelligence course. Agu Bot is a personalized career guidance chatbot created to align users' personalities, as determined by the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), with appropriate career paths that match their skill sets. The bot offers personalized development suggestions to bridge existing skill gaps through specific courses and experiences. Furthermore, Agu Bot provides users with educational content on different job roles and industries, aiding them in making well-informed career decisions. Essentially serving as a IT career counselor, Agu Bot streamlines the career planning process and assists users in discovering rewarding job opportunities that are well-suited to their individual profiles.

Role

Chatbot Developer

Responsibilities

As a member of the development team utilizing If-then rule and Dialogflow, my responsibilities included collaborating to establish rules for classifying different professions and devising questions to distinguish among MBTI types, soft skills, and hard skills. I was also tasked with creating intents to capture keywords from user interactions and generate appropriate responses.
