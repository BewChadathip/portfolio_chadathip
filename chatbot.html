Project Name

Agu Bot: A Career Guidance Chatbot Aligned with MBTI and Skill Sets

Project Details

Agu Bot is a personalized career guidance chatbot, designed to align users' personalities, based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), and skills with suitable career paths. It provides individualized development recommendations, bridging skill gaps with targeted courses and experiences. Additionally, Agu Bot educates users about various job roles and industries to support informed career decisions. Essentially, it acts as a digital career counselor, simplifying career planning and helping users find fulfilling job opportunities tailored to their unique profiles.
